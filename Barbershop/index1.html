Our Shop

Welcome to The Plaza Barbers!


The barbershop was once a place for men to go and not only get a great haircut and a hot-shave, but hang out with other men and escape the pressures of life for a few minutes.

In 2004, The Plaza Barbers, LLC. was established with the belief that men needed to have that option once again. You will notice the relaxed and social experience the moment you step through our doors.

We are located in Andover, Kansas. A few short minutes east of Wichita. We are proud to not only serve Andover, but the entire Wichita Metro area.
